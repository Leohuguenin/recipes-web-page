Wagyu Steak Tartare

Description

This Wagyu Steak Tartare is a refined take on the classic, delicate and flavorful dish made with high-quality Wagyu beef infused with bold Brazilian flavors.

Ingredients

  • 100g Wagyu beef (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp coriander (chopped)
  • 1 tea spoon parsley (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp pickled shallot (finely chopped)
  • A few dots of dendê mayo
  • 1 tsp sofrito
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Watercress (for garnish)
  • A drizzle of olive oil
  • A dash of Tabasco
  • A few pieces of cassava crisps

Dendê mayo is a Brazilian-inspired mayonnaise made with palm oil (dendê oil), giving it a rich, golden color and unique flavor.

  1. Make a mayo base using 3 egg yolks
  2. Add 1 red chilli and 5 tbsp palm oil
  3. Transfer to piping bags.

Cassava crisps are thin, crispy chips made from cassava root, a staple in Brazilian cuisine.

  1. Bring 500g of cassava to boil, until overcooked
  2. Add 50g of tapioca flour and blend in the thermomix.
  3. Keep adding water until not too sticky
  4. Spread over flat trays
  5. Dehydrate overnight
  6. Deep fry in very hot (smoky) oil

Preparation Steps

  1. Finely chop the Wagyu beef and place it in a mixing bowl.
  2. Add all ingredients except for the mayo and cassava crisps.
  3. Gently mix all ingredients until well combined.
  4. Scoop the mixture onto the plate and shape it freely with a spoon.
  5. Carefully add the mayo dots randomly.
  6. Garnish with watercress and serve immediately with cassava crisps.
