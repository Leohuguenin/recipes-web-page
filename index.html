Wagyu Steak Tartare

Description

This Wagyu Steak Tartare is a refined take on the classic, delicate and flavorful dish made with high-quality Wagyu beef infused with bold Brazilian flavors.

Ingredients

  • 100g Wagyu beef (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp coriander (chopped)
  • 1 tea spoon parsley (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp pickled shallot (finely chopped)
  • A few dots of dendÃª mayo
  • 1 tsp sofrito
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Watercress (for garnish)
  • A drizzle of olive oil
  • A dash of Tabasco
  • A few pieces of cassava crisps (randomly broken)

Preparation Steps

  1. Finely chop the Wagyu beef and place it in a mixing bowl.
  2. Add all ingredients except for the mayo and cassava crisps.
  3. Gently mix all ingredients until well combined.
  4. Scoop the mixture onto the plate and shape it freely with a spoon.
  5. Carefully add the mayo dots randomly.
  6. Garnish with watercress and serve immediately with cassava crisps.
